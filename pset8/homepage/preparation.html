This is how you prepare Yerba Mate!

Just follow this easy recipe and you can enjoy your own Mate!

Ingredients:

  • Your own mate (this can be from Wood, grapefruit, metal, plastic.. all give different tastes to the yerba)
  • Straw (or Bombilla)
  • Water boiler (preferably with a temperature control, but others work too)
  • Yerba (look at the buy part of this website for different types)
  • Water (amount depends on how much you (and others) want to drink, generally go with 1 liter)
  • Optional: Thermos to keep the water at the right temperature
  1. First start heating up the water to around 70C (158F) (the same temperature that is ideal for green tea)
  2. Pour yerba into the mate until about 2/3rd is filled
  3. Put your hand over the top of the mate, and shake it a little so the dust goes out
  4. Tilt the mate to a side so the yerba is in a diagonal line down from the top
  5. Optional: Pour the heated water into a thermos
  6. Fill the mate with (heated)water on the side where the yerba is lower
  7. Put your finger on top of the Bombilla, and push it into the mate on the side you poured the yerba
  8. Enjoy your fresh cup of mate
  9. When empty refill from the thermos or straight from your water boiler
  10. Optional: after a while the yerba might lose taste so you should refill part of it!
