Mate has a ton of health benefits!

These are some of the benefits when you drink mate:

  • Rich in Antioxidants and Nutritions
    • It has even more antioxidants than green tea and many helpful nutritions
  • Boosts energy and improve mental focus as it has more caffeine than tea but less than coffee
  • May lower blood sugar levels and reduces complications of diabetes
    • Yerba mate may help lower blood sugar and reduce the complications of diabetes. In fact, a recent animal study reports that it may improve insulin signaling.
  • May reduce risk of heart disease
    • Similarly, animal studies seem to show that mate provides protection from heart disease. In humans is can reduce cholesterol levels.
